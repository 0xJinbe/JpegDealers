Services

What we offer

Our approach is not to sell you services, our approach is to sell you value that will take your project to the next level. All our services include a free consultancy session, you can schedule your introduction meeting here.

Advisory & Launchpad

Whether you’re a first-time founder or a seasoned veteran, each project comes with its own challenges. Our team is comprised of successful builders and founders with a track-record of selling out projects from scratch, able to help you through the technical and operational components of scaling your project and delivering your vision.

‍JPEGDEALERS offers two primary consultancy streams: general advisory and Launchpad*.We’re here to support projects regardless of size or stage and are happy to connect with new founders and experienced builders alike.
‍
‍*To ensure our Launchpad projects have a maximum chance of success, we currently limit the number of projects in the Launchpad at any given time and cannot guarantee acceptance. Please enquire about availability if you’re interested in being considered for the JPEGDEALERS Launchpad.‍

Marketing & Collabs

We've teamed up with the best marketing and collab specialists in the space. Giving us the network power of over 200 established projects which can be initiated for collabs. We also offer a custom marketing solution for projects we develop, giving your project the edge over the others. Marketing in the current atmosphere of the space is of paramount importance and that's where we can deliver.

Custom Smart Contracts

Our team has extensive experience building and auditing in Web3. We offer custom contract solutions – ranging from ERC721A minting contracts and ERC20 tokens, to innovative burning contracts and play-to-earn experiences. With a focus on security and efficiency, our contracts will ensure your users will have an incredible experience, everytime.

Whether you’re looking for simple minting, whitelisting, and staking solutions, or more complex, custom contracts, our team can deliver.

Generative Art Engine

In a crowded NFT space, art is more than just a JPEG. Your art is often the first touch point investors experience when they encounter your project. At JPEGDEALERS, we’ve created a flexible generative art engine to deliver robust collections. With the ability to create multiple generations, variations, and rarities, our art team can help ensure your project stands out and meets your unique requirements.

Web3 Website Development

Our experienced JPEGDEALERS developers can help integrate and build comprehensive Web3 features onto your project’s website. Common services include minting pages, custom marketplaces, staking portals (with internally or externally developed smart contracts) and API building.

Whatever your requirements, our development team is equipped to build you the Web3 website of your dreams.

Generative VX Collections (Cross-chain)

Whether you’re a new project building hype, or an existing project looking to provide additional value to your holders, introducing a VX Collection is a great way to engage your community. JPEGDEALERS’ VX Collection service includes modelling, API development, 3D viewing, and generative rendering. Our team can build a Sandbox compatible VX collection from scratch or build a VX collection based on existing NFTs.

Discord Setup and Bots

For our clients we offer a wide variety of discord options such as custom bots, price trackers, ticket systems, raffle and whitelist setups, we also offer a full service package where we setup the complete discord, design it and setup the bots according to your needs.
